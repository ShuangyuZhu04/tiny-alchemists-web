My Contributions & Technical Work

🏃

Player Movement & First-Person Controller

Programmed the complete first-person player movement system, including momentum-based horizontal movement at ~3 units speed, a single-height jump with vertical impulse reaching ~1 unit, and slightly floaty gravity tuned specifically for platforming control. Implemented air control so players can adjust trajectory mid-jump, and ensured the physics-based movement interacts correctly with potion effects like zero-gravity zones and bouncy surfaces. The movement is network-synchronized so all connected players see consistent, smooth motion.

CharacterController Physics First-Person Network Sync
⚗️

Core Alchemy & Brewing System

Implemented the potion brewing pipeline according to the GDD recipe validation rules: checking for at most 1 base ingredient, at most 3 modifier ingredients, and no duplicates. When a valid recipe is brewed, three identical potions pop out of the cauldron; invalid recipes trigger a cauldron explosion with knockback and fire effects. Built the BaseEffect / ModifierEffect architecture supporting all 6 modifiers (Fire, Size, Float, Bouncy, Magnetic, Sparkle) combined with 3 base forms (Cloud, Object, Puddle), including special interactions like Fire + Sparkle creating repeated explosions and Bouncy + Magnetic causing repulsion.

Alchemy System Recipe Validation Effect Pipeline C#
🎯

Player Interaction & Inventory System

Built the complete player interaction system using raycast-based detection within ~1 unit range. Players can Pick Up holdable objects (ingredients, potions, plant pots) into a 3-slot inventory, Drop items with a gentle forward toss, or Throw them with enough force (~10 units) to break potions on impact. Implemented the IInteractable interface for cauldrons and other world objects, and the Holdable component with visual staff-levitation feedback. Inventory slot switching is supported via number keys and mouse scroll.

Raycasting Inventory IInteractable Holdable
🧪

Playtesting & Physics Debugging

Conducted extensive playtesting sessions to verify physics consistency across all potion combinations, ensuring interactions like magnetic attraction, bouncy collisions, and zero-gravity zones produce predictable yet entertaining results. Identified and fixed collision issues including objects clipping through cauldrons, player-object penetration during pickup, and slope movement instabilities. Tuned the "slightly floaty" gravity feel to balance platforming precision with the chaotic fun the game targets.
