My Contributions & Technical Work

🏃

Player Movement & First-Person Controller

Programmed the first-person player movement system, including momentum-based horizontal movement at ~3 units speed, a jump with vertical impulse, and slightly floaty gravity tuned specifically for platforming control. Implemented air control so players can adjust trajectory mid-jump, and ensured the physics-based movement interacts correctly with potion effects like zero-gravity zones and bouncy surfaces. The movement is network-synchronized so all connected players see consistent, smooth motion.

⚗️

Core Alchemy & Brewing System

Implemented the potion brewing pipeline according to the recipe validation rules: checking for at most 1 base ingredient, at most 3 modifier ingredients, and no duplicates. When a valid recipe is brewed, potions pop out of the cauldron; invalid recipes trigger a cauldron explosion with knockback effects. Built the architecture supporting all 6 modifiers (Fire, Size, Float, Bouncy, Magnetic, Sparkle) combined with 3 base forms (Cloud, Object, Puddle).

🎯

Player Interaction & Inventory System

Built the player interaction system. Players can Pick Up holdable objects (ingredients, potions, plant pots), Drop items with a gentle forward toss, or Throw them with enough force (~10 units) to break potions on impact. Implemented the interface for cauldrons and plantpot.
